  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Flavorings (optional): vanilla, cinnamon
Instructions:
  1. Mix dry ingredients: flour, sugar, yeast.
  2. Combine wet ingredients: warm water, milk, butter.
  3. Knead dough: until smooth and elastic.
  4. Let rise: 1 hour or until doubled.
